# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In short

- Be respectful. Disagree with the idea, not the person.
- Assume good faith. Most "wrong" PRs have a real concern hidden in them.
- Keep technical discussions technical. Personal attacks and harassment
  are not welcome.
- If you see behaviour that breaks this, raise it with the maintainers
  privately (open an issue with the `conduct` label, or email
  `kidandcat@gmail.com`).

The maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, lock threads, or
block contributors whose behaviour repeatedly disrupts the project.
